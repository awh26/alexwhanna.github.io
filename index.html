About me

My name is Alex Hanna and I am a graduate student at Purdue University working towards an M.S. in Aeronautics and Astronautics. I completed my B.S. in Aeronautical and Astronautical Engineering in December 2023 with minors in Computer Science, Economics, and Mathematics at Purdue. This website is meant to highlight some of the cool projects I have worked on.

Dragonfly V2

About

The second iteration of the Dragonfly is an autonomous glider designed for the NASA Balloon Program Office. It was completed and delivered to NASA's Columbia Scientific Balloon Facility in Palestine, TX in June 2024. It will prospectively be launched in Antarctica in Jan 2025.

Dragonfly V1

About

DRAGONfly is an autonomous glider designed to deliver a data vault from high altitude weather balloons to ground stations. This vehicle was designed for the NASA Balloon Program Office as a part of the FLOATing DRAGON competition beginning in August 2022. After submitting a conceptual design in January 2023, the DRAGONfly was selected as one of the six finalist vehicles to be built for a test flight in August 2023.
